    Our Products

    We specialize in the design, implementation and provision of platforms for
    Electronic Payments, Collections, Bills Payment and Funds Disbursement.

    XpressPay

    Our payment gateway for the collection
    of payments from customers using
    cards, bank accounts and eWallets
    without having to redirect customers to
    another site for payment to be made.

    Xpress Agent

    Xpress Agent is designed to provide
    access to financial services to everyone
    through engaged agents under a valid
    agency agreement.

    Pay Xpress

    PayXpress is a one-stop Value Added
    Service (VAS) platform designed to
    provide customers with easy access to
    pay bills.
